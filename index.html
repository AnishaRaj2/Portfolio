To-Do List

Github Demo

This is my first React project, a user-friendly todo list. With this application, you can easily add and delete tasks. Additionally, when a task is completed, you can mark it as done. The beauty of this todo list is that all tasks are saved in the browser's local storage, ensuring that you won't lose them even if you close the browser.

Powerful Vegan Life

Github Demo

This is my recipe project where I am collecting all our family's trusted recipes. What sets this recipe website apart from others is the unique layout I'm implemplanting. In this project, I display the ingredient list and cooking steps side by side, eliminating the need for excessive scrolling. With this design, users can easily reference the ingredients while following the cooking instructions without having to scroll back and forth multiple times.

Project is still work in progress.

A little bit about myself

I am a Front-end/Web developer based in Helsinki, Finland.

I am curious, creative and collaborative person, who loves to solve logical puzzles and hunt for bugs in UI designs. I am a very detail-oriented person with a service design mindset. I am always striving to step out of my comfort zone, to explore and expand my knowledge and skills.

"I believe that value is created together and through the integration of creative design and technology, we can develop products that are intuitive, practical, and accessible to all."

I'm a self taught developer and have learnt a lot of the processes, concepts and theories of UX/UI and product design through attending workshops, reading blogs, taking online courses like Fitech, Helsinki university MOOC courses, etc and applying these to projects in my spare time and at work.

When I'm not coding or designing, you'll often find me seeking solace and rejuvenation amidst the captivating Finnish nature known as "Luonto" - a place where I find inner peace. And when I'm not exploring the great outdoors, you can catch me at home, enjoying quality time with my family. I have a deep passion for capturing the beauty of nature through the lens of my camera. Another interesting fact about me is that I'm always fascinated by the intricate connections between the mind, body, and the food we eat!
