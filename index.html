A little bit about myself

I am a Front-end/Web developer/Tester based in Finland.

I enjoy designing and coding user interfaces that are not only visually stunning but also user-friendly, responsive, and accessible. It's the creative aspect of this work that truly resonates with me. My keen attention to detail leads me to discover and squash UI bugs, ensuring a seamless experience for users. I am a curious person, who is always ready to learn something new. Collaborating with others is another source of inspiration for me, as I believe that the best solutions often emerge through teamwork.

Currently i am self studying by doing various online courses, mooc courses, summer courses, etc and applying all my learnings into personal projects. I'm an active participant in the tech community. I make it a point to attend events and meetups, where I get the chance to connect with fascinating individuals, engage in insightful discussions, and stay up-to-date with the latest tech trends and conversations.

I'm someone with a empathic and compassion heart. My passion lies in helping people by offering support, motivation, and a genuine ear to listen. I thrive on finding creative solutions to the challenges others face, aiming to make their journeys a bit smoother and brighter.

When I'm not coding or designing, you'll often find me seeking solace and rejuvenation amidst the captivating Finnish nature known as "Luonto" - a place where I find inner peace. And when I'm not exploring the great outdoors, you can catch me at home, enjoying quality time with my family. I have a deep passion for capturing the beauty of nature through the lens of my camera.
